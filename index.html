About the Hackathon

We’re a team of EPFL students passionate about quantum science, from algorithms to hardware.
Join us for a fun weekend of building, learning, and tackling cool quantum challenges alongside brilliant, like-minded people (and possibly winning some cool prizes!). We welcome participants from all backgrounds. New to quantum? No problem. Your curiosity is enough, come hack with us!

Practical Information

Location & Access

The hackathon will take place at EPFL, BC building.

EPFL is accessible via the Lausanne Metro M1.

From Lausanne station, take the M1 onward Renens and exit at EPFL. The campus and hackathon venue are a short walk from the station.

The SwissTech Convention Center hotel is within walking distance.

Logistics

  • For participants who come from outside of Switzerland, we consider partial reimbursement of travel costs to Lausanne.
  • Meals and refreshments will be provided throughout the three days of the event.
  • Participants may coordinate shared accommodation and find potential hosts in the WhatsApp group (Coming Soon).
